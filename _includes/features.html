Search

Easily search for new addons across multiple providers. Including Curse.com and WoWInterface.

Install

At the click of a button have an addon downloaded and installed, ready to use. Works for addons kept in Git, SVN, and Mercurial too.

Update

Update a single addon, or all at once. Automatically download the latest version and install it.

Delete

Don't want that addon anymore? Let firestorm delete it and all of its stored data.

Backup

Just in case things go wrong, firestorm will backup your addons and their data for you. Easily create backups, restore backups and schedule automatic backups.

For Free

firestorm is open-source and free to download and use.
